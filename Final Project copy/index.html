Why Books?

Throughout my entire life I have always loved to read, but with the responsibilities of work and school it is difficult to find time to sit down with a good book. To make more time for books I have completed this project on my favorite books!

Fiction or Non Fiction?

Both! I love to read all kinds of books, spanning from historical fiction, to comedies, to informational. Mostly I just like to learn new and interesting information or ideas.

My Favorite Places to Get Books

Going to a local library is most often the best place to find new books to read. My favorite library is the Boston Public Library, but my local libary (The Concord Public Library ) is a great resource as well.

When I want to buy a book my first choice is always Gibson's Book Store in Concord. They are an awesome local independent bookstore!
